Should We Dine in Sydney?

Type a Sydney location, pick a cuisine, and let the internet pretend to be decisive for once.

This app searches for restaurants in Sydney and shows Google ratings, address, phone number, and a suggested thing to eat based on the cuisine. Because making lunch decisions manually is apparently beneath us now.
