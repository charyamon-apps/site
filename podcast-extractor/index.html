Podcast RSS Transcript Extractor

Paste a podcast RSS feed, load the episodes, choose one, then transcribe it. Slightly more magical. Slightly more likely to be offended by dodgy feeds.

Use the RSS feed, not the Spotify or Apple Podcasts page. Those are glamorous dead ends.
